If you've spent time working with Gemini, you've probably wished there was a faster way to input prompts. Typing slows down your thinking, especially when you're crafting complex questions or long-form requests. Your fingers can't keep pace with your ideas.

**Typeless** changes that. It's an AI-powered voice dictation app that transforms your speech into text in real-time, making prompt input to Gemini up to **6x faster** than typing.

## What Is Typeless? — The Next Standard in Voice Input

Typeless isn't just another speech-to-text tool. It uses **deep learning models to convert your voice with high accuracy**, and adds intelligent auto-correction and formatting features on top.

### Key Features

- **6x Faster Input**：Get text on screen up to 6 times quicker than manual typing
- **Automatic Cleanup**：Removes filler words like "um," "uh," and "like" automatically
- **100+ Language Support**：Handles Japanese, English, Mandarin, Spanish, and more
- **Personal Dictionary**：Learn custom technical terms like Gemini, API, prompt engineering, and MCP
- **Privacy First**：Optional on-device processing keeps your voice data on your phone
- **Smart Formatting**：Auto-converts speech into clean lists, paragraphs, and structured text

## Getting Started — 3 Simple Steps

### Step 1：Install the App

Typeless is available on both iOS and Android.

- **iOS**：Search for "Typeless" on the App Store and install
- **Android**：Find "Typeless" on Google Play and install

### Step 2：Initial Setup — Language & Dictionary

Once installed:
1. Go to "Settings" → "Language" and select your preferred language
2. Open "Personal Dictionary" and add technical terms you use often (Gemini, API, prompt, MCP, etc.)
3. Turn on "Filler Removal" (enabled by default)

### Step 3：Connect to Gemini

Open Gemini in your browser → tap the text input field → tap the Typeless icon to start voice input.

When done, hit send.

## Language Accuracy — How Does Japanese Perform?

Typeless offers strong Japanese language support, with **recognition accuracy above 98%** for standard Japanese.

Keep these points in mind:

- **Mixed Formality Levels**：Sometimes you'll mix formal and casual speech. Minor manual tweaks may be needed
- **Technical Terms**：Pre-register industry terms in your personal dictionary for best results
- **Punctuation**：Automatically inserted, but complex sentences may need a quick review

## Privacy & Data Security

Typeless gives you control over how your voice is processed:

1. **Cloud Processing (Default)**：Highest accuracy and fastest response. Voice data is sent to servers
2. **On-Device Processing**：All processing happens on your phone. Maximum privacy

For sensitive or confidential information, **on-device processing is strongly recommended**.
