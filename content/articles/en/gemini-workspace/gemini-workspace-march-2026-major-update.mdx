## Google Workspace Just Got a Major AI Upgrade

In March 2026, Google rolled out one of the most significant Gemini-powered updates to Google Workspace yet. The update brings new AI creation and editing features to Google Docs, Sheets, Slides, and Drive — fundamentally changing how you create content and work with data.

The headline feature is that **Gemini can now pull information from your files, emails, and the web to generate personalized content**. Instead of starting from a blank page, you describe what you need, and Gemini drafts it for you using your own data as context.

In this article, we'll walk through each new feature, show you how to use them in practice, and explain what these changes mean for your workflow.

## "Help Me Create" in Google Docs

The standout addition is the **"Help me create" tool** in Google Docs. Available from the side panel or a new bottom bar, it lets you describe what you want in plain language, and Gemini generates a personalized first draft by referencing your relevant files.

### How It Works

Type a prompt like this into the "Help me create" panel:

```
Draft a newsletter for our neighborhood association using the
meeting minutes from my January HOA meeting and the list of
upcoming events.
```

Gemini automatically searches your Google Drive for the referenced files, extracts the relevant information, and produces a structured draft. It can also pull context from your Gmail messages and Calendar events, saving you the effort of manually gathering information.

### Refining Your Document

Once you have a draft, you can refine it with natural language instructions:

- **"Make this doc more professional while keeping the tone energetic"** — adjust the overall tone
- **Highlight a section and say "make this more concise"** — edit specific passages
- **"Strengthen the call to action in the conclusion"** — enhance specific elements

### Match Writing Style and Match Doc Format

The **"Match writing style"** feature unifies the voice across your entire document. This is especially useful for documents written by multiple contributors — Gemini harmonizes everything to sound like one consistent author.

**"Match doc format"** applies the formatting structure (headings, bullet styles, spacing) from a reference document. Perfect for aligning reports to company templates without manual reformatting.

## "Fill with Gemini" in Google Sheets

Google Sheets introduces **"Fill with Gemini"**, a tool that dramatically speeds up table population, data classification, and summarization tasks.

### What You Can Do

With "Fill with Gemini," you can issue natural language instructions to populate your spreadsheet:

```python
# Example "Fill with Gemini" prompts:
# "Categorize the product names in column A into column B"
# "Summarize the reviews in column C into column D"
# "Fill column E with the latest market price from Google Search for each product"
```

The most powerful aspect is that **Gemini can pull real-time information from Google Search** directly into your cells. This means you can run competitive pricing research, gather market data, or verify facts — all without leaving your spreadsheet.

### Benchmark Performance

Google reports that Gemini in Sheets has achieved a **70.48% success rate on SpreadsheetBench**, a public benchmark designed to evaluate AI models on real-world spreadsheet editing tasks. This demonstrates that the AI is reaching a level of reliability suitable for meaningful data work.

## Auto-Generated Presentations in Google Slides

Google Slides has received a significant upgrade as well. When you ask Gemini to create a slide, it now handles:

- **Smart messaging** — crafts text that effectively conveys your ideas
- **Automatic layout design** — creates layouts with proper hierarchy, spacing, and visual balance
- **Style matching** — ensures new slides match the design of your existing deck

Looking ahead, Google has announced that **full presentation generation from scratch** is coming soon. You'll be able to say "Create a quarterly review presentation using my project data" and receive a complete, on-brand deck built from your Workspace data in seconds.

## Cross-App Search and Insights in Google Drive

Google Drive's Gemini integration now enables **cross-app search and analysis**. When you select sources, Gemini can connect information from your files, emails, and the web to surface insights.

### Practical Example

Ask something like "Summarize the progress on Project X from last month" and Gemini will:

1. Search your Drive for related documents
2. Extract relevant information from Gmail threads
3. Supplement with current web information
4. Generate a unified summary report

This eliminates the need to open files one by one and manually piece together information — a major time saver for project managers and team leads.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Is this available in languages other than English?

A: As of March 2026, the new Docs, Sheets, and Slides features support English only. Drive features are US-only beta. Support for additional languages including Japanese is expected within a few months, though no official timeline has been announced. You can still use the features by writing prompts in English.

### Q: Can I use these features with a free Gemini account?

A: No. The new features require Google AI Pro ($19.99/month) or higher. Free Gemini accounts retain access to the original "Help me write" functionality. See our [plan comparison guide](/en/articles/gemini-basics/gemini-free-vs-pro-ultra-which-plan-2026) for details.

### Q: What's the difference between "Help me create" and "Help me write"?

A: "Help me write" was limited to generating and editing text within a single document. "Help me create" is a significant upgrade — it can **pull data from multiple sources** across your Drive, Gmail, and Calendar to generate contextualized, personalized documents. The output is far more practical because it's grounded in your actual data.

### Q: How does Google handle data privacy with these features?

A: Google has confirmed that Workspace data is not used to train AI models. For enterprise Gemini Alpha customers, data handling complies with organizational security policies. File access follows your existing Google Drive sharing permissions — Gemini can only reference files you already have access to.
