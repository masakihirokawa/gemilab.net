As of March 2026, Google AI employs a 4-tier subscription structure designed to meet every need from individuals to enterprises. This guide comprehensively covers all four plans—Google AI Free, Plus, Pro, and Ultra—covering pricing, features, available services, and practical selection criteria.

## Complete 4-Plan Comparison

| Plan | Monthly Price | Gemini Model | Context | Storage | Key Additional Services |
|------|--------------|--------------|---------|---------|------------------------|
| **Free** | Free | Gemini 2.0 Flash | Standard | 15GB | Basic Gemini chat |
| **Plus** | $7.99 | Gemini 2.0 Flash | Extended | 100GB | Enhanced Gemini, Google Workspace AI |
| **Pro** | $19.99 | Gemini 3.1 Pro | 1M tokens | 2TB | NotebookLM Plus, Jules, Veo 3, Deep Search, Enhanced Workspace AI |
| **Ultra** | $249.99 | Gemini 3.1 Pro + Deep Think | 1M+ tokens | 30TB | All Pro features + Deep Think, Project Mariner, Veo 3.1, Jules 20x limits, Google Cloud credits |

## Gemini Models: Detailed Specifications

### Gemini 2.0 Flash (Free/Plus)
- **Release**: December 2024
- **Characteristics**: Fast, lightweight, optimized for real-time processing
- **Reasoning precision**: Standard level
- **Use cases**: Daily questions, basic coding support, content generation
- **Response time**: Average 0.5–1 second

### Gemini 3.1 Pro (Pro/Ultra)
- **Release**: March 2025
- **Characteristics**: Advanced reasoning, large-scale context processing
- **Context window**: 1 million tokens (equivalent to ~1,500 A4 pages or ~30,000 lines of code)
- **Reasoning precision**: Advanced. Excels at complex math, programming, multilingual processing
- **Use cases**: Large document analysis, complex code analysis, research paper synthesis
- **Response time**: Average 2–5 seconds (depends on complexity)

### Deep Think (Ultra exclusive)
- **Reasoning approach**: Visualizes step-by-step thinking process
- **Reasoning time**: Seconds to minutes depending on task complexity
- **Accuracy improvement**: 30–40% accuracy boost (measured on complex problems)
- **Use cases**: Math problems, logic puzzles, complex coding challenges, academic research
- **Processing load**: High (requires dedicated resources, Ultra only)

## NotebookLM: Feature Comparison Across Plans

NotebookLM is Google AI's "AI research notebook" that loads multiple documents and provides integrated AI analysis.

### Free Plan
- **Notebook creation**: Not available

### Plus Plan
- **Maximum notebooks**: 50
- **Sources per notebook**: 50 each
- **Daily chat limit**: 100
- **Audio generation**: 3 per day max
- **Use cases**: Simple document organization, basic Q&A, small projects

### Pro Plan (NotebookLM Plus)
- **Maximum notebooks**: 500
- **Sources per notebook**: 300 each
- **Daily chat limit**: 500
- **Audio generation**: Unlimited
- **Features**: Cross-reference functionality, auto-detected references across notebooks
- **Use cases**: Enterprise research, complex investigations, academic support

### Ultra Plan (NotebookLM Ultra)
- **Maximum notebooks**: Unlimited
- **Sources per notebook**: Unlimited
- **Daily chat limit**: Unlimited
- **Audio generation**: Unlimited
- **Advanced features**: Priority processing, advanced relationship detection
- **Use cases**: Large-scale enterprise research, continuous document management

## Jules: AI Coding Agent

Jules is an asynchronous code execution agent developed by Google AI that automatically executes user-specified tasks.

### Free/Plus Plans
- **Access**: Not available

### Pro Plan
- **Concurrent tasks**: Up to 3
- **Monthly executions**: Up to 100
- **Executable operations**: File creation, editing, deletion, code generation, bug fixes
- **Context**: Uses Gemini 3.1 Pro (1M tokens)
- **Use cases**: Routine code fixes, small-to-mid feature implementation, PR review assistance
- **Execution time**: Average 5–30 seconds per task

### Ultra Plan (Jules 20x Higher Limits)
- **Concurrent tasks**: Up to 30
- **Monthly executions**: Up to 2,000
- **Executable operations**: Pro operations + multi-agent execution
- **Workflow execution**: Complex workflow design and execution
- **Use cases**: Large project automation, microservices development, DevOps automation
- **Execution time**: Optimized to average 3–20 seconds per task

## Veo: Text-to-Video Generation

Veo is Google AI's high-quality video generation service, creating videos from text prompts.

### Free/Plus Plans
- **Access**: Not available

### Pro Plan (Veo 3)
- **Quality**: 1080p, up to 16 seconds
- **Generation speed**: Average 30 seconds to 2 minutes
- **Monthly credits**: 1,000 (video generation = ~10–50 credits depending on length)
- **Whisk Animate**: Convert static images to video
- **Use cases**: Marketing videos, social media content, tutorials, animation
- **Output format**: MP4 (H.264)

### Ultra Plan (Veo 3.1)
- **Quality**: 1440p, up to 60 seconds, voice narration support
- **Voice generation**: AI narration, auto-generated BGM
- **Monthly credits**: 25,000 (about 25x Pro)
- **Priority processing**: Dedicated GPU resources, 20–30% faster generation
- **Use cases**: Fully automated video production pipeline, high-quality corporate videos, voiced tutorials

## FAQ

**Q: Which plan for Deep Think's cost-effectiveness?**

A: Deep Think delivers 30–40% accuracy improvement for math, algorithms, and complex logic. ROI depends on your field. Data scientists, researchers, and advanced developers benefit. General business users may not justify $249.99/month.

**Q: Can multiple users co-edit NotebookLM notebooks?**

A: Not currently. Notebooks are single-user owned, with read-only sharing via email. Multiple users require separate Pro contracts.

**Q: How reliable is Jules' auto-code?**

A: Jules achieves 90%+ success on routine tasks (CRUD, API calls, simple algorithms). Complex security features, multi-language projects, and deep dependency fixes require human review.

**Q: How does mid-month plan switching affect billing?**

A: Monthly plans auto-renew. Changes trigger prorated billing. Upgrade mid-month = difference charged the same month.

**Q: Does Workspace AI use storage quota?**

A: No. AI features in Docs, Sheets, Slides don't consume storage. Only files in Drive do. Videos from Veo do consume storage, requiring adequate Pro/Ultra capacity.
