## Introduction — A New Way to Explore Eastern Astrology with AI

**Sanmei-gaku** (算命学) is an ancient form of Chinese astrology that has been practiced in East Asia for thousands of years. Unlike Western astrology, which focuses on the positions of planets and constellations, Sanmei-gaku derives its readings from the **Heavenly Stems and Earthly Branches** (a sexagenary cycle) and the **Five Elements theory** (Wu Xing), using your date of birth to reveal your core personality, talents, and life trajectory.

Traditionally, mastering Sanmei-gaku requires years of study. But with Google's AI assistant **Gemini**, you can experience this fascinating system of divination through a simple prompt. In this guide, you'll learn the fundamentals of Sanmei-gaku and discover exactly how to get personalized readings from Gemini.

## What Is Sanmei-gaku? — The Essential Foundations

Sanmei-gaku is a system of fate calculation rooted in the ancient Chinese philosophy of **Yin-Yang and the Five Elements** (陰陽五行). While Western zodiac signs are determined by birth month, Sanmei-gaku converts your **exact birth date into a combination of Heavenly Stems and Earthly Branches**, then analyzes the balance of the Five Elements to understand your nature.

### The Ten Heavenly Stems (十干 / Jikkan)

The Ten Heavenly Stems are the core building blocks of Sanmei-gaku. Each one is linked to one of the Five Elements and carries either a Yin or Yang quality.

| Stem | Japanese | Element | Yin/Yang | Image |
|------|----------|---------|----------|-------|
| 甲 | Kinoe | Wood | Yang | A towering tree |

| 己 | Tsuchinoto | Earth | Yin | A garden |

| 辛 | Kanoto | Metal | Yin | Jewels and gems |
| 壬 | Mizunoe | Water | Yang | A great river |
| 癸 | Mizunoto | Water | Yin | Rain and dew |

### The Twelve Earthly Branches (十二支 / Jūnishi)

These are the familiar twelve animals of the Chinese zodiac — Rat, Ox, Tiger, Rabbit, Dragon, Snake, Horse, Goat, Monkey, Rooster, Dog, and Boar. Each also corresponds to one of the Five Elements.

### The Birth Chart (命式 / Meishiki)

In Sanmei-gaku, your birth **year, month, and day** are each converted into a pair of Heavenly Stem and Earthly Branch (from the 60-unit sexagenary cycle). Together, these three pairs form your personal **birth chart** (命式).

The most important element is your **Day Stem** (日干 / Nikkan) — the Heavenly Stem of your birth day. This represents your **core self**: your fundamental personality, temperament, and how you relate to the world.

### Important Limitations to Keep in Mind

While Gemini has learned extensively about Sanmei-gaku theory, there are a few caveats:

- **Calendar conversion accuracy**: Dates near the boundary of a solar term (the day when months shift in the Chinese calendar) may produce slightly different results than a professional reading. For precise readings, cross-reference with a published perpetual calendar (万年暦)
- **Differences between schools**: Various schools of Sanmei-gaku interpret certain elements differently. Gemini's readings follow mainstream interpretations
- **Divination is for reflection**: Treat AI-generated readings as a tool for self-reflection and entertainment rather than definitive life guidance
