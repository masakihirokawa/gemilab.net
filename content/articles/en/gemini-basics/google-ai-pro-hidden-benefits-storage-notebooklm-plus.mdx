## More Than Just Gemini Advanced

Are you using Google AI Pro ($19.99/month) solely for Gemini Advanced? If so, you're leaving a lot of value on the table. The plan bundles several powerful perks beyond the AI chatbot — from generous cloud storage to upgraded research tools.

In particular, three benefits tend to fly under the radar: 2TB of Google storage, NotebookLM Plus, and 1,000 monthly AI credits. This guide breaks down each perk and shows you how to get the most out of them.

## Perk #1 — 2TB of Google Storage

### Your Google One Storage, Included

Google AI Pro comes with **2TB of Google One storage**, shared across Google Drive, Gmail, and Google Photos.

Google One's standalone 2TB plan costs $9.99/month. So if you're already paying for that, upgrading to AI Pro means you're essentially getting Gemini Advanced, NotebookLM Plus, and everything else for just $10 more per month.

### What 2TB Looks Like in Practice

```text
# What 2TB of storage gets you
Google Photos (high quality): ~400,000 photos
Gmail: 100+ years of normal email usage
Google Drive:
  - PDF files (1MB each): ~2 million
  - Word documents: ~1 million
  - Video files (1GB each): ~2,000

# Cost comparison
Google One 2TB standalone: $9.99/month
Google AI Pro: $19.99/month (2TB + Gemini Advanced + NotebookLM Plus + more)
Difference: $10.00/month ← All AI features for this price
```

For anyone who stores lots of photos, videos, or work documents, the storage alone makes AI Pro a compelling deal.

### Google Photos AI Editing Included

On top of the storage, AI Pro unlocks Google Photos' full suite of AI editing tools — Magic Eraser, sky color adjustments, portrait lighting effects, and more. These features used to be exclusive to Google One premium tiers, and now they're part of your AI Pro subscription.

## FAQ

### Q1: I already pay for Google One 2TB. Will I be double-charged if I upgrade to AI Pro?

No. Google AI Pro includes 2TB of Google One storage, so you can cancel your standalone Google One plan and switch to AI Pro without losing any data. Your stored files remain untouched. Manage the switch from the Google One settings page.

### Q2: What does Google AI Ultra ($249/month) add over Pro?

Ultra bumps storage to **30TB**, provides the highest-tier access to all models including Gemini 3.1 Pro, and significantly increases AI credits. It also unlocks Deep Research and Project Mariner (browser agent). That said, AI Pro covers the needs of most individual users.

### Q3: Can I subscribe to NotebookLM Plus separately?

As of March 2026, NotebookLM Plus is only available as part of Google AI Pro or Ultra plans. There's no standalone NotebookLM subscription. If NotebookLM Plus is your main motivation, AI Pro is the most cost-effective way to get it.

### Q4: Can I share the plan with family?

The 2TB storage can be shared through Google Family groups. However, AI features like Gemini Advanced and NotebookLM Plus are exclusive to the account holder. You can share storage with family while keeping full AI capabilities for yourself.
