## AI Isn't Just for Tech Experts

"Isn't AI for people who are good with computers?"

That's what many people think — but it couldn't be further from the truth. AI is now an everyday tool for anyone who uses a smartphone, and it's designed to make your life simpler and more enjoyable.

**Gemini** is Google's AI assistant. You probably already know Google from Search, Gmail, YouTube, and Google Maps. Gemini is Google's way of putting helpful AI into the hands of everyone — no technical background required.

## What Can Gemini Do?

Gemini helps with the small things that add up in daily life.

### Answer Your Questions

Unlike a search engine where you type keywords, Gemini lets you ask questions in natural, everyday language.

```
You: "What foods are good for high blood pressure?
      I want to cook something healthy for my mom."

Gemini: "Here are some heart-healthy foods:

         🥬 Potassium-rich foods
         • Spinach, bananas, sweet potatoes
         • Help your body flush out excess sodium

         🐟 Omega-3 rich foods
         • Salmon, mackerel, sardines
         • Support blood vessel health

         For a simple meal, how about baked salmon
         with a side of sautéed spinach?
         Would you like the full recipe?"
```

### Help You Write

Thank-you notes, email replies, community announcements — if putting words together isn't your strong suit, Gemini can draft messages that sound natural and thoughtful.

### Identify Things in Photos

Show Gemini a photo from your phone and ask questions: "What flower is this?" "How many calories are in this dish?" It can see and understand images.

## If You Have a Google Account, You're Ready

The wonderful thing about Gemini is that **if you already have a Google account (Gmail), you can start using it right now for free.**

```
How to start with Gemini:
1. Open your browser and go to gemini.google.com
2. Sign in with your regular Google account
3. Type a message in the text box at the bottom
4. Click the send button
5. That's it! Gemini will respond
```

No new accounts, no software to install. If you can check your Gmail, you're already set.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: I don't have a Google account. What should I do?

Creating one is free and takes just a minute. Go to gemini.google.com and click "Create account." You'll need a name and an email address (or you can create a new Gmail address).

### Q: How is Gemini different from Google Search?

Google Search shows you a list of web pages that match your keywords. Gemini understands your question and gives you a direct, summarized answer — saving you the work of reading through multiple websites.

### Q: Does it cost money?

The basic version of Gemini is completely free. For everyday questions and conversations, the free plan is more than enough. There's a premium tier called Gemini Advanced ($20/month), but start with free — no credit card needed.
