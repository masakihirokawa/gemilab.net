Google just released a game-changing feature: the ability to import chat histories from ChatGPT, Claude, and other AI services into Gemini. Announced on March 26, 2026, this feature makes switching to Gemini frictionless. Here's everything you need to know.

## What is Chat History Import?

The new import feature lets you transfer entire conversation threads from other AI platforms directly into Gemini. Your chat appears in the sidebar with full context preserved, ready to continue. This isn't copy-paste—it's genuine migration that maintains the conversation structure.

### What You Can Do

- **Import from multiple platforms**: ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, Perplexity, and more
- **Preserve conversation flow**: Questions, answers, and context stay intact
- **Continue conversations**: Ask follow-up questions and Gemini understands the prior context
- **Handle large batches**: Up to 5 zip files per day, 5GB total
- **Update existing chats**: Re-import the same conversation to overwrite with newer data

The feature is straightforward but has a few important considerations around region restrictions and quotas.

## Two Import Methods

### Method 1: Memory Import (Manual)

Summarize important parts of a prior conversation and paste it into a new Gemini chat.

**Steps**
1. Review the original conversation
2. Write down key takeaways and context
3. Start a new Gemini conversation
4. Paste your summary with a prompt like "Here's our previous conversation context..."
5. Gemini understands and continues from there

**Pros**: You control what information carries over, filtering out noise.
**Cons**: Time-consuming, loses detail, requires manual effort.

### Method 2: Chat History Import (Official Feature)

Export your chat history as a zip file from your current AI service and upload directly to Gemini. This is the new, preferred method.

**Supported Services**

- ChatGPT (OpenAI)
- Claude (Anthropic)
- Copilot (Microsoft)
- Perplexity AI
- Numerous others

## Step-by-Step: ChatGPT to Gemini

### Step 1: Export from ChatGPT

1. Go to ChatGPT (https://chatgpt.com) and log in
2. Click your profile icon (bottom left)
3. Select "Settings & Beta"
4. Choose "Data export" from the left menu
5. Click "Request export"
6. Check your email for a download link (may take hours to days)
7. Download the zip file containing all your chat history in JSON format

### Step 2: Import into Gemini

1. Visit gemini.google.com
2. Click the menu icon (≡) in the top left
3. Select "Import" or "Import chat"
4. Click "Select files"
5. Choose your ChatGPT export zip file
6. Wait for upload to complete
7. Your imported chats appear in the sidebar labeled "Imported chats"

### Step 3: Use Your Imported Chats

Imported conversations appear in your sidebar with a special import icon. You can:

- **Continue conversations**: Open an imported chat and ask new questions
- **Reference prior context**: Gemini understands the full conversation history
- **Re-import to update**: Upload the same zip again to overwrite with new data

## Step-by-Step: Claude to Gemini

### Step 1: Export from Claude

1. Go to Claude (https://claude.ai) and log in
2. Click your username or profile icon (bottom left)
3. Open "Settings" or "Account"
4. Find "Data & privacy" or "Download my data"
5. Request your data export
6. Wait for the email with a download link
7. Download the zip containing your chats in Markdown or JSON format

### Step 2: Import into Gemini

1. Go to gemini.google.com/import
2. Click "Select files"
3. Choose the zip file from Claude
4. The import begins automatically
5. Chats appear in your sidebar within minutes
6. Each imported chat shows an import icon for easy identification

### Step 3: Verify and Continue

Open a few imported chats and add new messages to confirm they work. Gemini will reference the prior conversation context when responding.

## Important Limitations and Quotas

### Regional Restrictions

Chat history import is **not available in**:

- **EEA (European Economic Area)**: EU member states and others
- **Switzerland**
- **United Kingdom**

If you're in these regions, use Method 1 (manual memory import) or consider a VPN solution.

### Daily Quotas

- **Maximum 5 zip files per day**
- **Maximum 5GB total per day**
- **Quotas reset daily**

Plan accordingly if you have large archives. You can split exports across multiple days.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: Can I edit imported chats?

The imported conversation history itself can't be edited, but you can add new messages. Gemini treats the imported history as context and builds from there.

### Q2: What happens if I import the same chat twice?

The newer import overwrites the previous one. This is useful if you've had new conversations with ChatGPT or Claude that you want to include.

### Q3: Can I delete imported chats?

Yes. Right-click an imported chat in the sidebar and select "Delete." This can't be undone, so only delete chats you're sure you don't need.

### Q4: Will my private chats import?

Privacy settings from the original service may not carry over. If you had private or protected chats, verify the export includes them before importing.

### Q5: Where is my data stored after import?

On Google's servers, accessible whenever you log into Gemini. Data is not stored locally—you can access it from any device.

## Tips for Getting the Most from Imported Chats

### Continue Complex Projects

If you were building something (writing code, brainstorming, planning) on ChatGPT or Claude, import the chat and continue with Gemini. Gemini picks up the context instantly.

### Consolidate Multi-AI Workflows

Using different AIs for different projects? Import everything to Gemini and manage it all in one place. You get one unified conversation library.

### Learn Gemini's Preferences

Once you import past conversations, Gemini learns your communication style and preferences. Future responses will align better with how you've previously interacted with AI.
