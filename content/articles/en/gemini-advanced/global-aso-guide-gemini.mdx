## Google Trends for ASO Keyword Research

### What Google Trends Reveals

Google Trends provides aggregated search data. For ASO, critical insights include:

1. **Search Trends**: Keyword search volume over 5 years
2. **Regional Trends**: Country-by-country search comparisons
3. **Related Keywords**: Terms searched in the same category
4. **Seasonal Patterns**: Search behavior by time of year

### ASO Research Application

**Example: Game App Keyword Research**

```
Search Keyword: "puzzle game"
Results:
- Global trend: Rising (2021-2026)
- By region: US (highest) > India > Brazil
- Seasonality: Peak winter (Dec), low summer (Aug)
- Related: match 3, brain game, casual game
```

Derived insights:
- Game app market is growing (worth long-term investment)
- India and Brazil markets show strong growth potential
- Winter campaigns are most effective
