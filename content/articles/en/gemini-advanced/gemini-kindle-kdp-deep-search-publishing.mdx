## Chapter 2: NotebookLM for Information Synthesis

### 2.1 What is NotebookLM

NotebookLM, developed by Google Research, is an AI note-taking assistant. Input multiple PDFs/URLs to automatically generate:
- Information summary
- Related questions extraction
- Systematic information organization
- Audio synthesis (podcast format)

For KDP writing, it accelerates research, information organization, and composition conception significantly.

### 2.2 NotebookLM Research Flow

**Step 1: Register Information Sources**

```
【Process】
1. Access NotebookLM (included with Google One Premium)
2. Click "+ Create Note"
3. Add information sources related to book theme:
   - Competitive work summaries (Amazon excerpts)
   - Academic papers (arXiv, Google Scholar)
   - Industry trend articles (online magazines)
   - Official guidelines (corporate/NPO publications)
   - YouTube videos (auto-transcribed)
```

**Example: "Freelancer Email Efficiency Book"**

```

   → Real reader concerns
```
