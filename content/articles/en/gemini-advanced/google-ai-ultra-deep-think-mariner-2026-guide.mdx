## Google AI Ultra: Complete Feature Guide

### 1. Deep Think — Advanced Reasoning Engine

Deep Think represents a new inference engine enabling deep thought on complex problems, providing more accurate and creative answers than instant responses.

**How It Works**

Deep Think decomposes problems into steps, validating at each stage—mirroring expert human reasoning.

**Use Examples**

Complex Math Problem Solving:
```
Problem: Prove √2 is irrational

Traditional Gemini: Returns standard proof immediately

Deep Think:
→ Examines multiple proof methods
→ Compares strengths/weaknesses
→ Provides beginner-friendly explanation
→ Suggests related concept extensions
```

Business Strategy Formation:
```
Challenge: Analyze SE Asia market entry obstacles

Deep Think:
→ Country-specific regulatory review
→ Cultural difference analysis
→ Competitive landscape deep dive
→ Comprehensive risk assessment
→ Staged entry strategy generation
```

**Performance**

- **Response Time**: 5-30 seconds (complexity-dependent)
- **Accuracy**: 40-60% improvement over baseline models
- **Creativity**: Brainstorming generates novel ideas
