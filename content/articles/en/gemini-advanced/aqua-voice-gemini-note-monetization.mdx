## Setting Up and Using Aqua Voice: A Step-by-Step Guide

### What is Aqua Voice?

Aqua Voice is a **high-accuracy Japanese voice recognition tool** built on Google's speech API. It supports extended audio recordings, automatically inserts punctuation, and offers real-time transcription.

### Initial Setup

1. **Sign Up**
   - Visit aquavoice.app
   - Log in with Google or Email

2. **Microphone Configuration**
   - Grant browser microphone permissions
   - Ensure a quiet recording environment

3. **Preferences**
   - Set language to Japanese
   - Enable "Advanced Mode" for improved accuracy

### The Basic Recording Process

1. Create a new project
2. Click the red record button
3. **Speak freely without worrying about structure** (just express your ideas naturally)
4. Speech is automatically transcribed to text
5. Review and correct any transcription errors

**Key Principle**: At this stage, **prioritize capturing ideas over perfection**. Editing comes later.

## The Aqua Voice → Gemini Workflow Architecture

### Three-Step Process Overview

```
[Step 1] Aqua Voice Recording (10–30 minutes)
     ↓
[Step 2] Gemini Advanced Structure, Research & Refinement (20–40 minutes)
     ↓
[Step 3] note Publishing & Optimization (10–15 minutes)
```

### Step 1: Free-Form Ideation via Aqua Voice

After selecting your article topic, use Aqua Voice to record your raw thoughts. It helps to keep this mental framework in mind:

- **Introduction**: Why you're addressing this topic
- **Background**: Current trends and problems
- **How-To**: Concrete techniques and methods
- **Case Studies**: Real success and failure examples
- **Call to Action**: What you want readers to do next

**Voice Recording Tips**:
- Speak clear periods (句点/。) so the system creates line breaks
- Say "・" when listing items
- Use conversational language; avoid overly complex expressions

### Step 2: Gemini Advanced Structure, Research & Refinement

Copy your Aqua Voice text and send this prompt to Gemini Advanced:

```
Please optimize the following voice transcription into a note article:

【Requirements】
- Structure with h2/h3 headings
- 2,000–3,000 characters of body content
- Naturally incorporate SEO keywords
- Explain technical terms on first mention
- Provide concrete examples and actionable techniques
- Include a clear CTA (call-to-action) in the final section

【Input Text】
{Your Aqua Voice transcription}

【Output Format】
Markdown format with this structure:
# Article Title
## Introduction
## Section 1
### Subsection
...
## Conclusion and CTA
```

Gemini Advanced then automatically:

- **Organizes your thoughts** into a logical structure
- **Fills content gaps** with supplementary information
- **Refines language** from conversational to polished
- **Optimizes for SEO** with keyword placement suggestions

### Step 3: Publishing & Optimization on note

Paste your Gemini-refined article into note and make these adjustments:

- **Title**: Include SEO keywords, keep under 80 characters
- **Body**: Insert 2–3 images (one per major section)
- **Hashtags**: 5–8 relevant tags for discoverability
- **Magazine Assignment**: Add to 2–3 relevant magazines
