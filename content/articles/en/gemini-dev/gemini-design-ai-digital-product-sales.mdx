## Introduction — Breaking Through the Design Barrier

In [Part 1](/articles/gemini-dev/gemini-multi-channel-monetization-basics), we explored five monetization channels powered by Gemini. Now in Part 2, we'll walk through the **complete process** of creating and selling digital products.

If you've ever thought "I can't create sellable products because I don't have design skills," this guide is for you. By combining Gemini's content capabilities with Canva and Figma's design tools, you can produce professional-quality products as a solo creator.

## Four Types of Digital Products That Sell

### Type 1: Template Collections

Business documents, social media posts, and presentation templates have consistent demand. Gemini designs the content structure while Canva or Figma handles the visual layer.

### Type 2: Guidebooks and Ebooks

Focused guides on specific topics are where Gemini Deep Research truly shines, delivering thoroughly researched, comprehensive content.

### Type 3: Checklists and Worksheets

Practical checklists and worksheets are relatively quick to produce, making them an ideal first product for beginners.

### Type 4: Design Asset Packs

Icons, banners, and social media templates created in Canva can be packaged and sold as design resource bundles.

## Building a Template Collection: Step by Step

### Step 1: Market Research with Gemini

```javascript
// Using Gemini API for market research
import { GoogleGenAI } from "@google/genai";

const ai = new GoogleGenAI({ apiKey: process.env.GEMINI_API_KEY });

const response = await ai.models.generateContent({
  model: "gemini-2.5-flash",
  contents: `Conduct digital product market research:
1. Top 5 Notion template categories on Gumroad by sales
2. Average price range for each category
3. Three niche angles for differentiation
4. Rank by ease of entry for beginners`,
});

console.log(response.text);
// → Market data and entry strategy output
```

### Step 2: Content Architecture with Gemini

Based on your research, have Gemini design the template structure — section layout, field definitions, and usage instructions can all be generated in one pass.

### Step 3: Design Production with Canva or Figma

Bring Gemini's structural blueprint to life visually.

**With Canva**: Select a template and customize. Set up brand colors and fonts once, and every product in your series will maintain visual consistency.

**With Figma**: More advanced customization is possible. Auto Layout and Components features elevate template quality significantly.

### Step 4: Building Your Sales Page

Stitch can auto-generate a landing page with product descriptions, feature lists, FAQs, and purchase buttons — all in minutes.

### Step 5: Setting Up Stripe Payments

For selling on your own site, you'll need Stripe integration.

```javascript
// Create a Stripe payment link
import Stripe from "stripe";

const stripe = new Stripe(process.env.STRIPE_SECRET_KEY);

// Create product
const product = await stripe.products.create({
  name: "Freelance Project Management Template Pack",
  description: "10 Notion-based project management templates",
});

// Set price
const price = await stripe.prices.create({
  product: product.id,
  unit_amount: 999, // $9.99
  currency: "usd",
});

// Generate payment link
const paymentLink = await stripe.paymentLinks.create({
  line_items: [{ price: price.id, quantity: 1 }],
});

console.log(`Payment link: ${paymentLink.url}`);
// → Generates a https://buy.stripe.com/xxx URL
```

## Pricing Strategy

Your pricing directly impacts revenue and perceived value.

**Entry Products ($5–$15)** — Low-priced items for first-time buyers. Single templates or checklists work well here. The goal is to build trust through a positive purchase experience.

**Core Products ($15–$50)** — Template collections and guidebooks that deliver substantial value. This is your primary revenue tier.

**Premium Products ($50–$150)** — Products with significant added value: video walkthroughs, consultation sessions, or comprehensive resource bundles. Fewer sales needed for meaningful revenue.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I create sellable products without design skills?

Absolutely. Canva provides professionally designed templates that you can customize by simply changing colors and text. Let Gemini handle the content structure and Canva handle the visuals — no design training needed.

### Should I sell on Gumroad, Etsy, or my own site?

For global reach, Gumroad offers low fees and specializes in digital products. Start with one platform, stabilize your sales, then expand to others.

### Is Stripe difficult to set up?

Creating a basic payment link requires just a few clicks in the Stripe dashboard — no coding needed. For API customization, Gemini can help you write the integration code.

### What should I create first?

Checklists or single-topic worksheets are ideal starting products. They're quick to produce, have steady demand, and give you a success experience to build on.
