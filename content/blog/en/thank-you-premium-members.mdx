## Important: Restoring Access on a New Browser or After Clearing Cookies

Here's something important all our members should know.

If you **switch browsers** or **clear your cookies**, you may temporarily lose access to premium content. The good news: restoring it takes just seconds:

1. **Visit the Support page** — Go to `/support`
2. **Enter your purchase email** — Type the email address you used when buying your membership via Stripe
3. **Submit** — Your premium access returns instantly

Simple and quick. If you have any questions, we're here to help.
